The award-winning RAW PUB, one of the city's most popular rooftop bars, is located high up on the 12th floor of World Trade Center, Kathmandu,
and features a variety of cocktails, nightly DJs and amazing views of the Capital City of Nepal from its cabana booths.


RAW PUB at World Trade Center in Kathmandu,Nepal has a modern and stylish look, offering a wide range of coffee specialities, long drinks, beer,
wine and delicious snacks. Specials such as the daily afterwork hour, regular live DJ performances and monthly changing culinary treats even pull local audience.
See sport events live on screen or see the elegant garden lounge for a drink.




"Minimum Age Requirement"

After 7pm, guests must be at least 21 years old to enter.
